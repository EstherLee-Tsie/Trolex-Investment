About Trolex Investment

Trolex Investment is an immigration and labor consultant company founded in 2015. We provide comprehensive immigration and labor application services, including work permits and resident permits. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with local mines and corporations, assisting them with the necessary documentation for staying in Botswana.

Mission

To provide efficient and reliable immigration and labor services, ensuring our clients' smooth integration into the Botswana workforce and business environment.

Vision

To become the leading immigration and labor consultancy in Botswana, known for exceptional service and commitment to clients' success.
