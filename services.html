Short Term Permits Application

Commonly Known as emergency work permits used for employees to come to the country to work as soon as possible and can be used as temporary permit whilst the long term work permit is in process and they range from:

*One(1) day to fourteen(14) days.

No Government fee required

*15 days to three months respectively.

Government fee = P1500.00

Long Term Permits Application

This permits duration is from four(4) months to five(5) years.

It's for people who are foreigners and have been employed by a local company or their companies bringing them for secondment in Botswana.

VISAS Application

We assist with applying VISAS for all applicants who come to Botswana but come from countries that do not allow them to come without VISAS.

Waivers Application

We assist in waiver application.

Waivers are legal documents given to all people who qualify and are approved to be covered by a resident document or they get it to tally it with a work permit for stay in Botswana.

Botswana Qualification Authority(BQA)

We assist those who want their qualifications verified.

BQA iS responsible for assessing all the applicants qualifications and approving them so that applicants are then allowed to proceed with ERB application which is requirement for permit applications.

Government fee : P2000.00
